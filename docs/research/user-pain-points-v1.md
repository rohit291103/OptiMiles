# Prioritization framework

Each pain point is scored on severity, frequency, impact on goal achievement, and MVP priority. Levels: **P0** must solve · **P1** high priority · **P2** future enhancement.

---

# Pain point catalogue

## P0-1: I don't know where to start
Users have an aspiration but no idea how to begin ("Which card? How many points? Is this even possible?"). Ecosystems are complex and fragmented; many abandon research before acting.
**Solution:** goal-first onboarding, goal feasibility analysis, guided strategy generation.

## P0-2: Too much conflicting information
Blogs, YouTube, Reddit and comparison sites recommend different cards — sponsored, generic, outdated, not personalized. Users lose confidence.
**Solution:** personalized recommendations, explainable reasoning, structured reward intelligence.

## P0-3: I don't know which card is right for me
Users compare dozens of cards without knowing which matches their goal; current tools rank cards instead of solving goals. Poor choices reduce reward potential.
**Solution:** goal-based recommendation engine; existing-card optimization; new-card suggestions only where necessary.

## P0-4: Reward calculations are too complicated *(severity: very high)*
Users can't accurately compute points earned, milestone bonuses, transfer values, time-to-goal. Manual calculations lead to wrong assumptions.
**Solution:** reward calculation engine, simulation engine, automated milestone tracking.

## P0-5: I don't know if my goal is achievable *(severity: very high)*
Users invest time and money without knowing whether the goal is realistic — no platform provides feasibility analysis. They overspend or abandon.
**Solution:** goal achievability score, timeline estimation, confidence indicator.

## P1-1: I don't know how to spend optimally
Multiple cards, unclear which to use per purchase; rates differ by category.
**Solution:** spend allocation engine, category optimization, monthly strategy recommendations.

## P1-2: I don't understand transfer partners
Bank/airline/hotel programs operate independently with different rules; users avoid valuable transfers.
**Solution:** transfer path visualization, ecosystem education, transfer intelligence.

## P1-3: I don't trust AI recommendations *(severity: high)*
Black-box AI reduces adoption.
**Solution:** explainable AI, transparent calculations, recommendation rationale.

## P2-1: Reward programs keep changing
Ratios, benefits, fees change; strategies go stale.
**Future:** reward news, change alerts, strategy refresh notifications.

## P2-2: I forget to track my progress
Balances scattered across banks/programs; motivation drops.
**Future:** progress dashboard, goal tracking, milestone reminders.

# MVP focus

Solve the five P0 problems exceptionally well: (1) help users get started with confidence, (2) determine goal achievability, (3) recommend the right card strategy, (4) automate complex reward calculations, (5) explain every recommendation clearly.

# Final product insight

The biggest competitor is not another rewards platform — it is **user confusion**. People will spend to achieve premium travel; what stops them is uncertainty ("Am I choosing the right card? Will this strategy work? Am I wasting my spending potential?"). OptiMiles replaces fragmented research with a single, explainable, goal-driven strategy.
