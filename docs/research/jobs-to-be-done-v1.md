**Purpose:** Identify the functional, emotional, and social jobs users hire OptiMiles to do. If a feature does not help complete a job, it should not be in the MVP.

---

# Core job statement

> Help me achieve my desired travel experience using the smartest possible credit card reward strategy.

Everything else is a supporting job.

---

# Functional jobs

| # | Job | Situation → desired outcome | Supporting features |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Determine whether my travel goal is achievable** | Have a goal → confidence-backed assessment within my timeline | Goal parsing, reward estimation, achievability score, timeline simulation |
| 2 | **Know exactly what I need to do** | Know the what, not the path → actionable execution plan | Recommended cards, spend allocation, transfer path, required actions |
| 3 | **Choose the right credit cards** | Hundreds of cards, expensive mistakes → personalized recommendations aligned to my goal | Recommendation engine, eligibility filtering, existing-card optimization, fee analysis |
| 4 | **Maximize reward accumulation** | I already spend monthly → every rupee moves me toward the goal | Spend routing, category optimization, reward simulations |
| 5 | **Understand why the strategy works** | AI recommends → I need to trust before acting | Explainable AI, transfer path visualization, rationale |

# Emotional jobs

1. "I want confidence that I'm making the right decision." — replace uncertainty with clarity.
2. "I want premium travel to feel achievable." — luxury travel no longer exclusive.
3. "I don't want to regret choosing the wrong card." — reassurance through transparent, data-backed recommendations.
4. "I want to feel like I'm making smart financial decisions." — every purchase intentional.

# Social jobs

Travel more comfortably; experience premium travel otherwise unaffordable; become the knowledgeable person friends ask for advice.

# Supporting jobs (secondary)

Compare multiple strategies · optimize existing cards before applying · estimate goal duration · understand fee trade-offs · learn ecosystems · track progress.

# Jobs explicitly NOT solved in the MVP

Expense tracking · budget management · investment advice · tax optimization · loan recommendations · travel booking · award seat searching · bank account aggregation.

# Success criteria

Users can confidently answer, within a single session: Can I achieve my travel goal? What should I do next? Which cards should I use? How should I spend? How long will it take? Why is this the best strategy?

> OptiMiles is not in the business of recommending credit cards. It is in the business of helping people achieve travel aspirations through intelligent reward strategies.
